About

The international status quo for educating student is severely outdated. The actual system hasn't been questioned in a long time, and no global action has been taken to enforce a new, more appropriate for the time, solution. That is, until now. Taking advantage of the technological revolution, EdBud provides personalized education to each student. The grade system will be abolished, and more choices will be available after the basic topics are completed. EdBud will focus on improving the person's thinking capability rather than improving knowledge in an unecessary field. It's finally time for a change.
